A Decade of Culinary Craft

Baked With Raw Flour, Deep Love, & True Integrity

We started out in Ahmedabad as a private sweet-making shop dedicated to sourcing fine organic flours. Today, we stand as one of Gujarat's premium patisseries, catering to elite weddings and dessert events.

Our vision is simple: to make every moment sweeter with absolute commitment to natural vanilla pods, cold-pressed dairy creams, and high-protein local mill grain flours. No fillers, no chemical preservatives.
